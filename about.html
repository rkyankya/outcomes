Our History

Dependable Outcomes Limited (DOL) is a privately owned Ugandan company established in 1999. Our three core business fields are civil works and heavy lifting equipment, healthcare supplies, and aviation consulting. Each field forms a separate division. We own earth moving and heavy lifting equipment for civil works and related services, and a state-of-the-art plant for manufacturing intravenous (IV) fluids, dermatological ointments, Ear, Eyes and Nose (EEN) drops and sprays.

Dependable Outcomes

The first division, Dependable Outcomes Ltd (DOL), does road construction and leases out earth moving machinery and heavy lifting cranes. It has a highly qualified in-house team of mechanics for maintenance of all our equipment at our workshop and in the field. With a wide range of heavy machinery and a dedicated technical personnel we have been able to serve several clients to their satisfaction in the last 20 years of operations. Our equipment is mostly hired by contractors doing road construction and other civil works, and many have come to rely on our products and services.

Dependable Health Supplies

The second division, Dependable Healthcare supplies (DHS), manufactures IV fluids, dermatological ointments, EEN drops and sprays for domestic and foreign markets at our fully automated plant. The division is managed by a team of professional in processing engineering, pharmacologists, pharmacists and lab technicians to make sure the products comply with the highest quality standards at all times.


Dependable Aviation Consulting

Lastly, the division of Dependable Aviation Consulting (DAC) provides services in airline management, aircraft chartering and leasing, and outsourcing spare parts and maintenance services. This division is under a team of aviation professionals with solid experience and track record in airline management and economics, and outsourcing different services for airlines and cargo operators in the East Africa and Central Africa regions.

Products

DOL owns a variety of heavy machinery such as bulldozers, motor graders, excavators, compactors/rollers, water bowsers, low beds, cranes for up to 50 tons lifting capacity, etc. Most of our earth moving equipment is of Caterpillar brand which is known world over for its reliability and performance under any weather conditions and terrain. Customers can hire or lease any of this equipment for either daily, short or long-term work in Uganda and neighboring countries.
